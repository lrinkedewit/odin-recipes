Tiramisu Recipe

Recipe

Tiramisu is one of my very favorite dishes. My mother always makes it for the family whenever we have a special event. Here, I will share her recipe. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • coffee
  • ladyfingers
  • yoghurt
  • whipcream
  • dark chocolate
  1. Make a big pot of coffee
  2. Line the bottom of your dish with chocolate
  3. Soak the ladyfingers in the coffee and remove them just before they become soggy (about 5 secs)
  4. Lay the ladyfingers neatly on top of the chocolate
  5. Add a layer of yoghurt/whipcream mix
  6. Now add another layer of ladyfingers
  7. Again, add a layer of yoghurt/whipcream mix
  8. Top with dark chocolate and voila!
